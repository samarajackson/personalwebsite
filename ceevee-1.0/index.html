About Me

Currently, I am currently a student at Coding Dojo! I am learning 3 stacks, including Python, Java, and MEAN. Though I had some experience, I wanted to further my software skills to fully pursue a fullstack software engineering role.

I had prior experience working as a Software Product Engineer at Locus Technologies. I depended heavily on my JavaScript and SQL skills to create customer-specific web applications. I was the technical lead for a proposal, invoice, and business planning application, which is used in over 200 locations nationwide. Software started as a hobby for me, but my previous project management skills and environmental engineering experience have helped me understand the users of my applications, which make my applications more user-friendly. I received an B.S. in environmental engineering from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. I taught myself how to code, with help from Codecademy classes, my coworkers, and my friends. I also have completed the Front End Developer Blueprint with Skillcrush to further my frontend engineering skills.
