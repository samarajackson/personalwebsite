About Me

Currently, I am a job seeker! I just finished up my learning as a student at Coding Dojo! In 3 months, I learned 3 stacks, including Python, Java, and MEAN. Though I had some coding experience, I wanted to further my software skills to fully pursue a fullstack software engineering role.

I had prior coding experience working as a Technical Solution Consultant at Ivalua. Mainly focused on completing Proof of Concepts by using a combination of SQL queries and configuration using a platform. Worked on the SQL databases for projects to manipulate data and fix issues for demos. Worked on Microsoft Cube reporting.

As Software Product Engineer at Locus Technologies. I depended heavily on my JavaScript and SQL skills to create customer-specific web applications. I was the technical lead for a proposal, invoice, and business planning application, which is used in over 200 locations nationwide.

Software started as a hobby for me, but my previous project management skills and environmental engineering experience have helped me understand the users of my applications, which make my applications more user-friendly. I received an B.S. in environmental engineering from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. I taught myself how to code, with help from Codecademy classes, my coworkers, and my friends. I also have completed the Front End Developer Blueprint with Skillcrush to further my frontend engineering skills.
